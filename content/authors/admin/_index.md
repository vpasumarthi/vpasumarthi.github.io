Viswanath Pasumarthi is a computational scientist and Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Greeley group at Purdue University. His research focuses on ab initio molecular dynamics, density functional theory, and multiscale modeling of heterogeneous catalysis for energy conversion and chemical manufacturing. He has developed open source tools such as PyCD and CatEnergy that link atomistic simulations, automation, and data driven analysis. He is particularly interested in industry roles that combine computational chemistry, materials modeling, and scientific software development.
